Stratum is an open-source ML system for large-scale agentic pipeline search ([arXiv:2603.03589](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.03589)), developed by the DEEM Lab (BIFOLD & TU Berlin). It compiles batches of agent-generated ML pipelines into optimized DAGs and executes them across heterogeneous backends, including a Rust-based runtime. I contributed rule-based rewrites to its logical optimizer.
